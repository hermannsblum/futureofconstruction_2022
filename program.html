    OTHER INFORMATION

    Registration for Social Events

    Please, use the form to register for the social events taking place at the time of the symposiums - i.e. pre-apéro at EMPA NEST, closing apero at Eberhard, and cross-sessions. 

    Arrival - Public Transport

    To get the ETH Campus Hönggerberg, the free ETH-Link bus leaves every 20 min from station "Haldenegg" close to the city center. You can also take bus lines 80, 69 or 37. When you arrive to the bus station at ETH Hönggerberg, orient yourself using the map below and look for signs to reach the symposium locations.

    Lunch

    We recommend the mensas in HCI building (same location as the coffee breaks) or the HPH building (see location overview) for your lunch. There are also multiple cafes and a supermarket on campus.
